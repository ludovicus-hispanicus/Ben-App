Detectron seneitivity

Detectron sensitivity determines how sure the AI needs to be to consider something a sign.
The larger the sensitivity is, the less false-positives the AI would do.
Small sensitivty number, like 0.1, would help when the image is blurry. However, it might cause a lot of false-positives.
We recommend using 0.5.
